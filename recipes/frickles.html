Ingredients

  • 32 dill pickle slices
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 large eggs slightly beaten
  • 2 tbsp pickle juice
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 cups panko crumbs
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill
  • Cooking spray
  • Ranch dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees. Let pickles stand on paper towles for 15 mins or until almost all liquid is gone
  2. Meanwhile, in small bowl, combine flour and salt. In another bowl, whisk eggs, pickle juice, cayenne, and garlic powder. Combine panko and dill into third small bowl
  3. Dip pickles in flour mixture to coat both sides; shake off excess. Dip in egg mixture, then in crumb mixture, patting to help coating adhere. In batches, place pickles in a single layer on greased tray in air-fryer basket. Cook until golden brown and cripsy, 7-10 minutes longer. Serve immediately, with ranch dressing if desired
