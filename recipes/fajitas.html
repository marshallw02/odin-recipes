Ingredients

  • 2lbs skirt steak
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 large bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Lime juice
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, lime juice, chili powder, and oil.
  2. Skice meat into 1/2 inch slices
  3. Marinate meat and veggies separately in a bowl for 2 hrs
  4. Heat up grill until its hot then drain marinade form the meat and veggies and grill for a few minutes
  5. Serve with sour cream, salsa, and cilantro
